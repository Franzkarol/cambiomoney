Can Brazilians have a checking account abroad?

Although technology has facilitated different processes in our daily lives, opening a current account abroad creates many doubts for those who reside in Brazil. There are several purposes for having an account in another country: for those who travel a lot or have plans to move to the destination, for those who invest or intend to invest abroad or even who want to have a reserve of money in foreign currency. With an account abroad, different processes can be facilitated, such as sending money to children who are studying in another country, paying a supplier abroad, making international purchases more easily, among others.
